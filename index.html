Research

Research Interests

SPaT/R2X-based AMR Navigation

Communication-assisted navigation for autonomous mobile robots at signalized crosswalks, using Signal Phase and Timing information to support time-aware crossing decisions.

Vision-Language Models for Intersections

Applying VLMs to understand intersection context, including pedestrians, vehicles, occlusions, and potential conflicts around crosswalk environments.

ROS 2-based Robotic Systems

Building navigation and safety-control pipelines with ROS 2, Nav2, LiDAR-camera systems, and simulation environments such as CARLA.

Projects

Research Projects

SPaT-Assisted AMR Crosswalk Navigation

A ROS 2-based navigation framework that integrates Nav2, R2X/SPaT messages, and a hybrid safety node for signal-aware mobile robot crossing at urban intersections.

ROS 2 · Nav2 · SPaT · R2X · AMR

VLM-based Intersection Context Understanding

A research direction for using intersection camera images and VLM reasoning to capture crosswalk-level context beyond traffic signal state alone.

VLM · Scene Understanding · Crosswalk · Safety

CARLA-based Intersection Scenario Generation

Simulation pipeline for collecting intersection images, traffic scenarios, pedestrians, vehicles, and metadata for downstream VLM evaluation.

CARLA · Simulation · Data Collection · VLM Evaluation

LiDAR-based Robot Navigation with Fast-LIO2 and Nav2

Integration of LiDAR SLAM and ROS 2 navigation components for mobile robot localization, mapping, and autonomous navigation.

Fast-LIO2 · LiDAR · ROS 2 · SLAM · Nav2
