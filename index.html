Research

Research Themes

SPaT/R2X-based Crosswalk Navigation

I develop communication-assisted navigation frameworks for autonomous mobile robots at signalized crosswalks. The goal is to use Signal Phase and Timing information to decide whether a robot can safely enter, wait, or stop before crossing.

Hybrid Safety Control with ROS 2/Nav2

I build ROS 2-based navigation pipelines that combine Nav2 commands, signal state, remaining phase time, localization, and obstacle information through a centralized safety layer.

VLM-based Intersection Understanding

I am extending my crosswalk navigation research toward vision-language models that can reason about pedestrians, vehicles, occlusions, and potential conflicts from intersection camera images.

Projects

Research Projects

SPaT-Assisted AMR Crosswalk Navigation

A ROS 2-based framework that integrates Nav2, R2X/SPaT messages, and a hybrid safety node for signal-aware robot crossing. The system compares vision-only signal interpretation with communication-assisted SPaT decisions under challenging outdoor conditions.

ROS 2 · Nav2 · SPaT · R2X · AMR · Crosswalk Safety

Traffic Light Perception for Mobile Robots

A perception pipeline for traffic signal recognition using RGB images, YOLO-based detection, color-state classification, and temporal smoothing. The module is designed to interface with the same navigation and safety layer used by the SPaT-based system.

YOLO · RGB Camera · Traffic Signal Recognition · ROS 2

CARLA-based Intersection Scenario Generation

A simulation pipeline for generating intersection scenes with pedestrians, vehicles, cameras, and scenario metadata. This direction supports future VLM evaluation for infrastructure-based intersection understanding.

CARLA · Simulation · Data Collection · VLM Evaluation

LiDAR-based Robot Navigation with Fast-LIO2 and Nav2

Integration of LiDAR SLAM and navigation components for mobile robot localization, mapping, and autonomous navigation. This work supports real-world AMR operation with LiDAR-camera platforms.

Fast-LIO2 · LiDAR · ROS 2 · SLAM · Nav2

Multi-Camera Pedestrian Trajectory Perception

A perception pipeline that combines YOLO, DeepSORT, Re-ID, and Homography Transform to improve cross-camera pedestrian ID consistency and recover full pedestrian trajectories across camera views.

YOLO · DeepSORT · Re-ID · Homography · ITS

Face Recognition-based Automatic Door Control

A KIST internship project that used motion detection, RetinaFace, and WebSocket communication to develop a face recognition-based access control prototype for automatic door operation.

RetinaFace · GMM · WebSocket · Access Control · KIST
